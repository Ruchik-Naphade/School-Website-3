School Achivements

   Ryan Group awarded Samson Daniel Award
Ryan Group awarded Samson Daniel Award

Ryan International Group was awarded the prestigious 'Samson Daniel Award' this year for commendable contribution to the cause of the elders of the society, at the awards ceremony organized by HelpAge India on the International Day of Older Persons. The award was presented by Dr. Vinod K Paul, Member NITI Aayog and accepted by Dr. Snehal Pinto, Director, Ryan Group.

   Leading School Chain (National) Award 2019
Ryan Group awarded Samson Daniel Award

Ryan International Group of Institutions received the ‘Leading School Chain (National)’ award, at the '14th edition of the World Education Summit' which was held in Delhi. The World Education Summit is a global platform for the stakeholders in the education sector to discuss the latest innovations and technology in education. The conference witnessed mentors pointing at the key drivers for future growth in the Indian as well as the global education sector.

   Ryan International Group of Institutions shines at the Mid-Day Excellence in Education 2019 Awards
Ryan Group awarded Samson Daniel Award

Managing Director - Madam Grace Pinto was honoured with the ‘Visionary Leadership in the Education’ Sector and our Mumbai and Pune Schools were awarded for Excellence in National Curriculum School. Group’s CEO - Ryan Pinto received the awards and presented his views on the topic- 'Are we fitting 20th Century Models in a 21st Century Environment, along with other panellists as part of the panel discussion. Ryan Pinto spoke about the survival skills of the 21st century and how they can be purposefully applied in a classroom, and how educators can strategise and plan towards achieving this objective.
